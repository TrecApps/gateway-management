Welcome to Trec-Apps (Test). Here, we offer a wide variety of inter-related apps and services designed to enhance your experience of online browsing above existing solutions. With a Trec-Account, you can make personal connections with your friends, professional connections with your colleagues from work, show off your creativity in ways other services won't allow.

Checkout the User Manager to get started.


This is NOT a production website. The suite of apps offered by Trec-Apps is still in development.

User Manager

Start you journey here by making a Trec-Account. Once you have a Trec-Account, you will be abe to:

  • Validate your account
  • Manage active log-on sessions
  • Set up Multi-factor authentication with either your email or with an authenticator app
  • Change your password
  • (Not yet) Manage your subscriptions
